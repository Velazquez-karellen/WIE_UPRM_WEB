Mission

Our mission is to encourage and inspire women to develop STEM careers and support our members by providing technical and professional development through seminars, workshops, and social activities. We take it upon ourselves to find and offer opportunities to support our main objective through collaborations with other organizations as well.

Vision

We envision a community of women engineers and scientists that will be productive in their work environment and society, focusing on technical development, professional networking, and active engagement in their community as well as personal growth. It is our goal to develop elite women that strive to be the best at what they do, providing a unique vision that not only contributes to the advancement of technology but provides diversity in the work field.

Objectives

  • Encourage and inspire high school students to pursue an engineering career through the STAR Program.
  • Increase the growth and diversification of women pursuing engineering degrees where they are strongly underrepresented.
  • Help our members develop their social, technical, and professional skills through various activities.
  • Create a sense of community within the organization through community service activities.
