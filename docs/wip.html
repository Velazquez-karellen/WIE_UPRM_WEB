IEEE PES (Power & Energy Society)

An entity comprised of women whose stories and positions in the industry help demonstrate the success anyone can achieve through dedication. This leadership team is supported by mentors and role models who share their inspiring stories. It fosters more diverse leadership by promoting professional development, creating connections, and educating women in the energy industry. Our goal is not only to increase the number of women in the energy industry but also to guide them towards holding leadership positions!

Women In Power (WIP)

Developing candidates for leadership positions through recognition and leadership education. Providing inspiration and education through mentors, role models, and success stories. Establishing support among institutions to provide opportunities for candidates in leadership positions.
